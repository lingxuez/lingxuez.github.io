I am a third year Ph.D. student in Department of Statistics at Carnegie Mellon University. Before coming to CMU, I earned my B.S. in Statistics and minor in Economics from Peking University, China, in 2009.

My research mainly focuses on statistical genetics and high-dimensonal inference, under the supervision of Prof. Kathryn Roeder and Prof. Jing Lei. Currently I am working on hypothesis testing problems on gene networks, with hope to understand more about the genetic architecture of Schizophrenia.
